About Me

            I'm Liam O'Connor, a Business Economics major at the University of California, San Diego, hailing from the small coastal town of Dana Point, California. Growing up with four sisters has instilled in me the values of compassion, adaptability, and collaboration. My academic journey began at a Spanish-speaking school, where I navigated a unique educational experience by taking tests and writing essays in my non-native language. This early exposure to linguistic diversity not only sharpened my language skills but also fostered a deep appreciation for cultural nuances. It was during this time that I developed resilience and a passion for overcoming challenges, qualities that have become integral to both my academic and personal pursuits. I earned my Associates in Mathematics and Economics from Saddleback College, where my affinity for numbers and data analysis flourished. This academic foundation, combined with my experiences in a multicultural environment, has shaped me into a well-rounded individual with a global perspective. As the only brother to four sisters, one of whom has low-functioning autism, I have learned the importance of empathy, patience, and understanding. These qualities have been pivotal in shaping my communication skills, allowing me to connect with diverse audiences on a personal and professional level. My journey as a student-athlete, playing baseball at Saddleback College, reinforced the values of teamwork, discipline, and determination. Beyond the field, my commitment to excellence extends to my career aspirations in Financial Services, particularly in Investment Banking and Wealth Management. In addition to my academic pursuits, I work as a tour guide, leveraging my excellent communication skills to cater to diverse audiences. Proficient in both English and Spanish, I hold the Seal of Biliteracy, a testament to my dedication to mastering multiple languages and connecting with people on a deeper level. Technical proficiency is another facet of my skill set, encompassing Microsoft Office, Excel, MetroList (MLS), largescale CRMs, Stata, R, Studio, Matlab, Figma, and Adobe Products. My adaptability to new situations, coupled with a love for embracing discomfort as a pathfinder, sets me apart as a versatile and dynamic professional. Beyond academics and career pursuits, I find balance through my extracurricular activities. Frequent surfing, training in Jiu Jitsu, and a lifelong love for baseball showcase my commitment to personal growth and physical well-being. My passion for travel and learning languages reflects a broader curiosity about the world and a genuine interest in connecting with diverse cultures. In essence, my portfolio is a mosaic of academic achievements, personal skills, and extracurricular pursuits, woven together by the threads of a unique and enriching background. Thank you for exploring my journey, and I'm excited about the opportunities that lie ahead.
